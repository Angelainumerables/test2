Track Your Finances Easily

Luna is a versatile finance tracker designed to help users manage their expenses effectively.
With features that allow for easy bill splitting and expense tracking among friends and family,
Luna empowers individuals to take control of their finances by connecting their bank accounts and promoting better budgeting habits.
The app offers a seamless experience by automatically categorizing transactions and providing insights into spending patterns.
Users can create groups for shared expenses, making it simple to see who owes what in real-time. With robust security measures in place,
including encryption technology, Luna ensures that personal financial data remains safe. Whether you're on a trip or just managing daily expenses,
Luna makes financial management straightforward and user-friendly, allowing for both personal and shared finance tracking.
